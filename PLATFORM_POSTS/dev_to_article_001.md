# AI Can Generate Faster Than It Can Remember

AI-assisted work has a continuity problem.

Files drift. Chats forget. Patch chains rupture. Generated apps stop matching their own source route. Creative artifacts get flattened into contextless outputs.

Digital Mycelium v0.3 is my local deterministic prototype for that rupture.

It receives a file, ZIP, or folder; records structure; generates trace capsules and route receipts; exports a packet; re-ingests that packet; and verifies whether the route held.

The current branch has:

- hardened local review cockpit
- WSL/Linux second-platform witness
- public-safe demo packet
- top-of-chain manifest phase seal
- live demo rehearsal seal
- optional Ollama witness commentary

Boundary:

The model does not decide HELD.  
The deterministic kernel and receipts remain authority.

The ask:

Bring a route that usually breaks.
