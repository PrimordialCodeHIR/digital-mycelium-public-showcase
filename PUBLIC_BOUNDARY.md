# Public Boundary

Digital Mycelium does not claim:

- consciousness
- biological life
- AGI
- institutional endorsement
- external validation
- content settlement
- copyright determination
- legal advice
- ownership adjudication

The current public claim is narrower:

A local deterministic source-return engine can carry an artifact route through receipt generation, packet export, re-ingest verification, second-platform witness, and optional bounded local model commentary.

The deterministic kernel and receipts remain the authority.
