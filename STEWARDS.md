# Stewards

Digital Mycelium is not looking for gatekeepers.

It is looking for stewards who can pressure-test routes.

Bring:

- AI-assisted projects that drifted
- generated apps with broken patch history
- creative chains that got flattened
- source routes that disappeared
- provenance workflows that stop at metadata
- open-source contribution routes that lost continuity
- local-first audit trails that need receipts

The ask is not:

> believe the framework

The ask is:

> bring a route that usually breaks and let us test whether the receipt holds
