# Showcase Playbook v0.1

## Core line

The AI era does not just need more generation. It needs continuity infrastructure.

## Public frame

AI can generate faster than it can remember.

Digital Mycelium is a local deterministic source-return engine for carrying artifact routes through receipts, re-ingest verification, second-platform witness, and optional local model commentary without making the model the authority.

## Substrate inversion

Do not ask the tower to bless the route.

Build the route so clearly that the tower has to account for it.

## Contribution frame

Different route does not mean no contribution.  
No credential does not mean no cognition.  
Tool-assisted does not mean human-absent.  
Institutional distance does not mean void.

## What to show first

Show `DEMO_READY_STATE.md`.

Then show `TECHNICAL_RECEIPTS.md`.

Then run the cockpit.

## What not to lead with yet

- copyright coupling
- TED presentation
- legal claims
- institutional confrontation
- digital life claims

Those are downstream lanes.
